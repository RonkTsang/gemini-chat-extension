Welcome to the Gemini Power Kit! Once you have [installed](/guide/install/) the extension, navigating the features is simple.

## Accessing the Power Kit

When you open [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com), you will see a new icon in the top-left corner of the interface. This is your entry point to all the power features.

- **Check out [Chat Outline](/features/chat-outline/)** to navigate your long conversation threads.
- **Learn about [Quick Follow-up](/features/quick-follow-up/)** to run fast, contextual prompts on selected text.
- **Master the [Chain Prompt](/features/chain-prompt/)** feature for automating step-by-step tasks.
