Welcome to Gemini Power Kit! This guide will help you set up and start using your new productivity toolkit.


## 1. Installation

If you haven't installed the extension yet, please follow our [Installation Guide](/guide/install/).

## 2. Accessing the Kit

<Steps>
1.  **Open Gemini**: Navigate to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com).
2.  **Locate the Entry Point**: Look for the floating **Gemini Power Kit icon** at the top-left of the sidebar or main interface.
3.  **Click to Expand**: Click the icon to reveal the toolkit panel, giving you access to all core features.
</Steps>

## 3. Core Features at a Glance

### 🗂️ Chat Outline
Never scroll endlessly again. The **Outline** provides a structured map of your current conversation.
- Click any item in the outline to jump directly to that part of the chat.
- Use the **Pin** icon to keep the outline visible while you work.

### ⚡ Quick Follow-up
Turn selected text from a Gemini response into your next prompt without copy-pasting.
- **Select text in a Gemini response** to open the Quick Follow-up capsule.
- Click **Ask Gemini** to attach the selected text as quote context above the input box, then type and send your follow-up manually.
- Click a saved **Custom Prompt** to replace **Selected Text** in the template and send it automatically.

### 🔗 Chain Prompt
Automate repetitive, multi-step tasks.
- Go to the **Chain Prompt** tab in the toolkit.
- Select a pre-defined chain or create your own sequence of prompts.
- Click **Run** and watch Gemini execute the steps one by one.

### 🎨 Personalization
Make Gemini truly yours.
- Open the **Settings** (gear icon) in the toolkit.
- Choose from various **Visual Themes** and color presets.
- Upload a **Custom Wallpaper** and adjust the glassmorphism intensity.

## 4. Pro Tips

- **Smart Tab Titles**: The extension automatically syncs your browser tab title with your chat name. No more guessing which tab is which!
- **Stuff in New Tab**: If you use the "My Stuff" gallery, you can now open generated images or files in a new tab for a better view.
- **Privacy by Design**: Remember, everything runs locally. Your prompts and data are never sent to our servers.
