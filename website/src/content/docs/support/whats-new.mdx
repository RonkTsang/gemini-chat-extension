## v0.8.0 Reply Completion Notifications

*June 27, 2026*

Gemini Power Kit can now let you know when Gemini finishes replying.

<Image
  src={notificationSettings}
  alt="Notification settings for Gemini reply completion alerts, sound, custom audio, and troubleshooting"
  class="release-image"
/>

When a Gemini reply finishes in the background, Gemini Power Kit can send a system notification with the conversation title and a short preview. Click the notification to return to the right conversation, and the alert is cleared from the notification center.

Notifications are opt-in. The extension asks for the required browser permissions only when you enable the feature, and the settings panel includes a test notification plus troubleshooting guidance for browser and system notification settings.

### Notification Sound and Custom Audio

You can enable a completion sound for notifications, then upload your own audio if you want a more recognizable cue. Custom audio is stored locally by the extension and falls back to the bundled sound when no custom file is set.

### More Completion Types

The notification flow now handles more than basic text replies. It can recognize image results, generated videos, and Deep Research completion paths when Gemini exposes the final result state.

### Library Fix

- Fixed **Open in New Tab** in the Library gallery, so media items can open in a separate tab without losing your place in the gallery.

## v0.6.0 Themes and Wallpaper

*March 1, 2026*

This release turns Gemini into a workspace you can personalize without sacrificing readability.

<Image
  src={themeGuide}
  alt="Gemini Power Kit theme customization overview"
  class="release-image"
/>

Pick a color preset, and optionally add a wallpaper with adjustable blur and a glassy message look.

### Color Presets

<Image
  src={themeColors}
  alt="Theme color presets for Gemini Power Kit"
  class="release-image"
/>

With Color Presets, you can switch among curated themes in one click. Changes are reflected immediately, and your selection is saved automatically.

### Wallpaper

<Image
  src={themeWallpaper}
  alt="Wallpaper customization for Gemini Power Kit"
  class="release-image"
/>

A flat background is functional, but it can make long sessions feel visually fatiguing. Wallpaper helps you create a more personal workspace while readability controls keep the interface usable.

With Wallpaper, you can enable a background image and tune the blur for readability. Supported formats include PNG, JPG, and WebP.

With Message Glass Effect, you can toggle a glassy treatment for message containers to improve depth and style.

## v0.5.0 Smart Tabs, Gallery Enhancements

*February 4, 2026*

This update deepens the integration between your workspace and Gemini, making it easier to manage multiple threads and browse history without losing context.

### Smart Tab Titles

<Image
  src={smartTabTitles}
  alt="Gemini tab title synced to the current conversation"
  class="release-image"
/>

Working with multiple Gemini sessions often leads to a sea of tabs all named "Google Gemini," making it hard to distinguish research threads or coding tasks without clicking through each one.

With Smart Tab Titles, browser tab titles reflect the actual conversation or page you are on, reducing the time spent searching for the right tab.

### Open Media in New Tab

<video src={openInNewTabVideo} controls playsInline preload="metadata" class="release-video"></video>

Navigating historic resources on the "My Stuff" page previously felt restrictive. Clicking an item redirected the current page, and returning often meant losing scroll position and context.

The new Open in New Tab entry lets you review resources in new tabs while keeping your library gallery where you left it.
