Tab Title Sync ensures that every Gemini tab you open accurately reflects the conversation it contains, rather than just "Google Gemini".

## Features

- **Real-time Synchronization**: Automatically updates your browser tab title to match the chat conversation title.
- **Smart Detection**: Detects titles across different pages including Chat, My stuff, and Documents.
- **Zero Overhead**: Uses efficient event-driven logic (MutationObservers) with no background CPU polling.
- **Fallback Recovery**: Safely restores default titles when navigating back to the home screen or starting new chats.
