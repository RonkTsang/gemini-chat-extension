## Overview

Tab Title Sync automatically updates your Gemini browser tab title to the current conversation title.

## Why It Matters

When multiple Gemini tabs are open, they often show the same generic title, making tab management slow and error-prone:

- You cannot identify the right conversation at a glance.
- You waste time clicking tabs to find the target chat.
- Context switching across projects becomes harder.

This feature aligns tab titles with actual conversation names so multi-tab Gemini workflows stay fast and predictable.

## Key Capabilities

- Identify the right Gemini tab immediately.
- Avoid confusion when many Gemini chats are open.
- Keep tab names aligned as conversation titles change.

## Quick Workflow

1. Open a Gemini conversation.
2. Wait for title generation/update in Gemini.
3. Check your browser tab title.
4. Use tab titles to switch to the target conversation faster.
