Theme allows you to personalize Gemini's interface with a curated selection of color presets and background options.

## Features

- **Color Presets**: Choose from multiple built-in color themes to match your personal style.
- **Live Preview**: See your changes instantly in the settings panel before applying them globally.
- **Seamless Transitions**: Smoothly switch between themes with elegant UI transitions.
- **Automatic Persistence**: Your theme selections are saved automatically and synchronized across all your devices.
