The Chat Outline is designed to give you a bird's-eye view of your long and complex Gemini conversations.

## Features

- **Clickable Outline**: Generates a navigable list of all your prompts in a conversation.
- **Easy Navigation**: Click any prompt in the outline to instantly scroll to that point in the chat history.
- **Pin Mode**: Keep the outline popover open and visible while you scroll and interact with Gemini.
- **Smart Organization**: Automatically outlines nested structures and prompts so you never get lost.
