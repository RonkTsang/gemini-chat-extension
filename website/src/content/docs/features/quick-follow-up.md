Quick Follow-up empowers you to take action on any text in your Gemini conversations with a single click.

## Features

- **Text Selection Actions**: Highlight text anywhere and instantly access a menu of custom prompts.
- **Customizable Prompts**: Create your own quick-action templates tailored to your needs (e.g., Translate to Spanish, Explain this code, Summarize the points).
- **Template Library**: Gain access to a pre-built library to get started immediately without setup.
- **One-Click Execution**: Turn any selected text into a follow-up question effortlessly.
